The premier phytochemical database, phytochemdb.com, represents an authentic repository site for plant-derived phytochemicals that can ameliorate Computer-Aided Drug Design, Lead Optimization, Virtual Screening, and Molecular Docking as well as in the bioinformatics sector.
Currently, {{ n_plants }} plants from the Asian territory and their corresponding {{ n_compounds }} phytochemicals are available in this database that will escalate promptly.
This database assembles the Molecular Formula, 3D structure, Canonical SMILES, Molecular Weight, Hydrogen Bond Acceptor Count, Hydrogen Bond Donor Count, Molar Refractivity, Topological polar surface area (TPSA), WlogP, and Lipinski rule of five with the number of violations for each compound.
Anyone can inquiry about phytochemicals by the unique accession number, Canonical SMILES, Molecular formula, and plant names as well. Phytochemicals obtained from each plant can be found all together by simply searching the name of the plant.
This plant-derived phytochemical database will be immensely efficient for the researchers, professors, and learners of bioinformatics who are inquisitive at computer-aided Drug Designing, Lead Optimization, Visual Screening, and Molecular Docking.
Researchers who have screened phytochemicals in their laboratory and already published their data can contribute to this database. They can upload the three-dimensional structure of the phytochemicals in this database and the new uploaded compound will get a unique accession number after passing the review procedure.
